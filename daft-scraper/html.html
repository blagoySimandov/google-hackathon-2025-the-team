About this property

Description

Beautifully presented three bedroomed townhouse offering modern living in a peaceful residential enclave featuring a carport to the side with a sizeable private rear garden. This is an ideal family home in one of Castleknock’s most sought after developments. This home offers a modern design with kitchen, a spacious living room and double bedroom on the ground floor with two large double bedrooms on the 1st floor. This home offers convenient access to public transport bus links and Coolmine Rail Station, neighbourhood shopping centre, M50/N3 motorway, Blanchardstown Shopping Centre and numerous schools and parks. Viewing is highly recommended and by appointment only. Accommodation Entrance Porch: with hotpress and under-stairs storage, alarm control panel Lounge: feature fireplace with marble surround and wooden mantle, TV point and French doors to rear garden 4.9x3.8m Kitchen: modern fitted kitchen with wall and floor mounted units, tiled splash-back, tiled floor, plumbed for washing machine and dishwasher, patio door to rear garden Bedroom 3: with fitted wardrobes 2.9x2.7m Bathroom: with bath, W.C and W.H.B, partially tiled walls and tiled floor 1st Floor Bedroom 1: with fitted wardrobes 4.5x3.1m Ensuite: with shower, W.C and W.H.B, hot towel rail, partially tiled walls Bedroom 2: with fitted wardrobes 3.5x3.0m Outside Carport to front with parking for two cars Large rear garden laid out in lawn with fenced boundary Paved patio area Services GFCH Alarm system PVC double glazed windows Maintenance fee c. € Local Area Information Public transport routes that service Annfield: Bus: 37 and Rail: Clonsilla Rail Station Nearby Areas: Carpenterstown, Clonsilla, Coolmine and Blanchardstown Centre
